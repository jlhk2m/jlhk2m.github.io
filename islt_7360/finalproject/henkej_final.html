Teacher Introduction

My name is Jessica Henke and I am very excited to be your child's teacher this year. This is my 5th year in education and 3rd year as a full-times teacher in a special educaiton classroom. I have had the opportunity to work and observe in several classrooms in the Greater St. Louis Area. I have provided special education services in many different classrooms and settings; including but not limited to elementary and high school levels. I also have experience as respite and summer camp support staff with Community Living, Inc. Prior to this school year, I provided ABA services as a paraprofessional for 2 years, had the opportunity to spend my 1st year of teaching in an elementary functional skills classroom within Special School District, and 1 year as an Essential Skills teacher as a local high school.

My husband and I recently celebrated our 3rd wedding anniversary and live in the O'Fallon, MO area with our two dogs. We are both teachers in St. Charles County. We do not have children of our own, our students are our children. When is not in session we are traveling, making the most of our time together.

Classroom Information

At The Lead School, parents and caregivers will find a wide range of educational programs and services to support the inclusion of children with special needs. Mrs. Henke's Classroom has students between Kindergarten and 5th grade. These students are all working towards returning to their home district to participate in their Least Restrictive Environment (LRE). Each day we follow a modified general education curriculum in Language Arts, Math, Science, and Social Studies in order to meet the needs of each student as well as make progress toward their Individual Education Plan (IEP) goals.
