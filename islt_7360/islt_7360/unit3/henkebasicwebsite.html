Mrs. Henke's Basic Webpage

This is the first webpage that I have ever created. I feel so excited!!!!
As I think about the topic of my final website, here are some possibilities:

Topic 1

An Integrated Classroom Webpage

One of my ideas is to develop a website for my classroom to be integrated with the school webpage. An integrated classroom website would provide an additional avenue for the parents of my students to get involved, 5 Reasons for Website Integration:

  • It's easier than ever
  • It can create positive inter-classroom competition
  • It makes your brand more consistent
  • It improves parent engagement
  • It can boost your social media efforts

Topic 2

An Independent Classroom Webpage

The second idea is to develop a website for my classroom independent of the school's website for only the parents of my students. This independent website would protect my students rights as well as share information with their families. The website would provide information directly related to the activities and topics in my classroom specifically for the parents of my students. Topics such as:

  • Welcome!
  • Newsletters
  • Syllabus and Additional Materials
  • Classroom Calendar
  • Photo Gallery
  • Contact Me
